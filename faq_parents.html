How to you the App

Start using

1. How do I start using schooltek?

The information stored at schooltek is owned by the school. To start using it your school will add you in the application, after that you will receive an email with your user ID and password.

2. How do I connect with school information?

You can access the information of your children using schooltek webside or the Schooltek´s App on Apple Store or Google Play.

Security

1. How safe is the Schooltek´s App?

The schooltek´s app uses encryption and the highest standards in security to keep your data safer. The entire community uses a unique identifier of each school, username and password. Nobody else but the students and the parents can access their informations.

Communicating

1. Can I send messages to school using Schooltek?

Using Schooltek you can only receive messages from school. You can only confirm messages received from your school.

2. How do I connect to schooltek?

Schooltek can be used on any computer with Internet connnection or from its mobile App.

3. My phone must be in a specific network?

No. Your phone can be in any network.

4. Can I make voice calls from schooltek?

No, You can not.

5. Can other students access my academic informations using Schooltek?

No, they can not, the students and their parents can only access their own information.

6. From whom will I receive messages?

You will only receive messages from teachers or administrative staff.

How to you the App

1. How often can I download the mobile App?

It can be downloaded at any time.

2. Does Schooltek cost anything?

Yes, it does, but only for the school.

3. What levels of schooling is designed Schooltek?

Schooltek is designed for schools from kindergarten to university.

4. What smartphones does Schooltek App Support?

It supports iOS (iPhone 5, 5S, 6, 6 +, 6S and 6S +) and Android phones from version 4.1 (Jelly Beam) including Samsung, LG, Motorola, HTC and others.

5. Can I cancel schoolteks app?

Yes, you can at any moment.
