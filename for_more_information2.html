Schooltek provides an excellent online solution that allows parents to be aware of academic information to support their children to improve their school´s performance.

INSTITUTIONAL INFORMATION

The institutional panel displays information as Mission, Vision, Values, Rules and Directory.

Messages and Alerts

Parents and students receive messages with relevant school issues.

Tasks

Receive and download files with assigned tasks and display the delivery date. Download files to your phone or computer.

Privacy and security

Schooltek uses strong encryption to keep data safer and secure on our platform. The informations stored at schootek will only be seen by your school´s community and not by other schools. The informations will only be seen by students and their parents.

Assistance

Stay informed of students missing classes. Schooltek confirms when a student did not attend or delay.

Grades

Schooltek, informs parents in real-time of their children´s grades

Calendar

Schooltek synchronizes the official school´s calendar with your phone as well as confirming attendance or non-attendance at school´s events.

Tuitions

Schooltek uses the simplest and the easiest way to generate the school´s tuition´s Billing. The parents could pay even using Schooltek´s mobile App.
