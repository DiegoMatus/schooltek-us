1. Where does Schooltek work?

Worldwide.

2. Why do you charge a monthly service fee?

Schooltek requires an internet connection to function. The monthly service fee helps us to cover expenses such as our cloud platform, data base infrastructure, backend computing, tech Support and App development.

3. How much does the service cost?

Schooltek is free for parents and students. The schools will start paying at least $1 USD per student begining in 2017.

4. What types of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards.

5. Do I need the subscription service for the Schooltek to work?

Yes, begining in 2017, the subscription service will be required.

6. Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can at any moment.
